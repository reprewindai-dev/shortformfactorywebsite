# ShortFormFactory — Product Copy

## Tagline
Turn one long-form idea into a week of short-form content — automatically.

## Hero Headline
One video. Seven days of content. Done.

## Hero Subheadline
ShortFormFactory turns any long-form content into a full week of platform-ready short-form clips, captions, and hooks — automatically.

## Short Description (PeerSpot / meta description)
ShortFormFactory is an AI-powered content automation suite that ingests a source video, podcast, blog post, or voice note and outputs a full week of platform-ready short-form clips, captions, hooks, and thumbnails. It handles the repetitive repurposing work so creators and marketing teams can focus on the original content.

## Full Description
ShortFormFactory connects to your existing content pipeline — YouTube, podcast RSS, Google Drive, or direct upload — and runs an automated repurposing workflow: transcription, highlight extraction, clip generation, caption writing, hook creation, and thumbnail copy output in a single job.

### Key capabilities
- Source ingestion: video, audio, text, or voice note
- Automated clip extraction with engagement scoring (identifies the highest-performing 30–90 second moments)
- Platform-specific formatting: TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, Twitter/X
- Hook and caption generation trained on high-performing short-form patterns
- Batch mode: process a full content library in one run
- White-label output for agency use

### Who it's for
Content creators, social media managers, marketing teams, and agencies producing short-form content at scale.

### Deployment
Web app (SaaS), mobile-ready.

**Engagement-first extraction**

### Value Prop 3
**Built for scale**

# FreestyleWriter — Product Copy

## Tagline
AI that writes with you, not instead of you. Built for rappers, writers, and lyricists.

## Hero Headline
Your flow. Amplified.

## Hero Subheadline
FreestyleWriter is the AI lyric assistant built for rappers and writers — it sparks, extends, and structures your raw ideas without erasing your voice.

## Short Description (PeerSpot / meta description)
FreestyleWriter is an AI lyric and creative writing assistant built specifically for freestyle rappers, songwriters, and spoken word artists. It doesn't write for you — it sparks, extends, and structures your raw ideas into polished verses while preserving your voice and flow.

## Full Description
FreestyleWriter is a lyric writing assistant that understands rap structure, flow patterns, rhyme schemes, and syllable timing. Unlike general AI writing tools, it is trained on freestyle and battle rap conventions and built to augment a writer's existing voice rather than replace it.

### Key capabilities
- Freestyle input to structured verse output with preserved cadence
- Rhyme scheme suggestions (AABB, ABAB, complex multisyllabic patterns)
- Bar extension: take a single bar and generate a full 16
- Flow analysis: identifies rhythm breaks and suggests fixes
- Ghost mode: zero AI fingerprint, matches your voice profile
- Export to PDF, MIDI timing grid, or plain text

### Who it's for
Rappers, songwriters, spoken word artists, and creative writing enthusiasts who want AI assistance that enhances rather than replaces their voice.

### Deployment
Web app (SaaS), mobile-ready.

## Value Props (website sections)

### Value Prop 1
**Trained on rap, not corporate filler**
Understands multisyllabic rhymes, bar structure, and freestyle cadence.

### Value Prop 2
**Extends your bars, not replaces them**
Give it one line, get back a full 16 in your voice.

### Value Prop 3
**Ghost mode output**
Zero AI fingerprint, just your words, structured.
