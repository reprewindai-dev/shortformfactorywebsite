What is ShortFormFactory?

ShortFormFactory is an AI-powered short-form video infrastructure platform. We automate the full production pipeline — from script generation to voiceover, video assembly, and multi-platform publishing — so creators and agencies can scale content without a team.

Is this just another SaaS tool?

No. ShortFormFactory is infrastructure. Think of it like AWS for short-form video — a full operating system (SFFOS) with modules for manufacturing, distribution, analytics, and monetization. It is built for scale from day one.
