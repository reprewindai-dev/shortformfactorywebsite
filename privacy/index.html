Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  |  RepreWind AI Dev / ShortFormFactory

This Privacy Policy explains how ShortFormFactory (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform at shortformfactory.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email address, and password (hashed)
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Payment data: processed securely by Stripe or PayPal; we do not store card numbers
  • Content data: scripts, prompts, and videos you create through the Platform
  • Analytics data: collected via Google Analytics (GA4) with consent defaults set to denied

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Platform
  • To process payments and send receipts
  • To send product updates and support communications (you may opt out at any time)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) to complete transactions
  • Infrastructure providers (Vercel, Railway, Supabase) for platform operation
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics) under data processing agreements
  • Law enforcement when required by law

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. Analytics cookies require your consent. You may decline non-essential cookies via our cookie banner or browser settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days except where required by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Submit requests to privacy@shortformfactory.com.

7. Security

We use TLS encryption in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest for sensitive data. Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel.
